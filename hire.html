HIRE ME

I am self-employed and make a living from training, mentoring, and freelancing. The ways I can be of service to you are usually through:

  • Training. If, as a team of developers in Europe, you need a workshop on React Native, RxJS, RxJava, reactive programming, functional programming in JavaScript, Cycle.js, or general JavaScript, I can organize a training (of a few days long) in person with the team. I have executed many such trainings in the past, and customers were very satisfied. Contact me preferably by email.
  • Mentoring. If, as a developer, you are "stuck" in some critical issue with RxJS, RxJava, reactive programming, functional programming in JavaScript, or Cycle.js, I can help you with a short mentoring session over a video-call. Contact me preferably through Codementor.
  • Contract work. If your software project needs a senior developer with experience in RxJS, RxJava, reactive programming, functional programming, Cycle.js, React Native, React, or frontend architecture, I am likely to help you by committing a few weeks or months to solving your problems. That said, my availability for projects is at the moment is very limited. Alternatively, I can recommend expert developers I know from my network. Contact me preferably by email.

For other types of work, feel free to send me an email and we can discuss the details and viability.
